Elements

The Idea

Elements is a 3D adventure-platformer focused on a large scale world and fully fleshed out universe. There will be four main areas (based on the Elements) with a number of level/zones to complete and explore. All of the creatures and zones in this world are a rapresentation or combination of the four Elements: Fire, Water, Air and Earth. The zones will test the ability of the player to traverse the zone, solve puzzles, and make his way through ancient enemies and dangers. The obstacles the player faces will vary from moving platforms and dangerous lasers to flying beams and more. Another important aspect of the game is exploration and collectibles, the player will collect various currencies that he will use to clear the way of locked portals and will be rewarded for exploration by the ability to disable certain obstacles based on the currency he has obtained on the way. The player will start in the Earth zone and as the story progresses he will face the Fire, Air and Water zones.

State of the Project

The project is in full development and a Demo level has been constructed to illustrate what the gameplay may feel like. The demo takes place in the Earth zone and is one of the first the player will face. As of writing this, the world terrain map is done and textured, the most important game systems such as player movement, combat stats, buffs, (prototype) UI, camera movement, items, respawns, and combat are mostly complete together with the systems required for a level (such as platforms, gates, portals, collectibles, beams and puzzle solving elements). All of the assets and models are custom made and the goal is too make all the assets ourselves whenever possible. If you are interested in seeing the world and some of the assets in game check out this demo: Demo.

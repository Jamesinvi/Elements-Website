About The Project

Elements is a 3D adventure-platformer focused on a large scale world and fully fleshed out universe. There will be four main areas (based on the Elements) with a number of level/zones to complete and explore. All of the creatures and zones in this world are a representation or combination of the four Elements: Fire, Water, Air and Earth. The zones will test the ability of the player to traverse the zone, solve puzzles, and make his way through ancient enemies and dangers. The obstacles the player faces will vary from moving platforms and dangerous lasers to flying beams and more. Another important aspect of the game is exploration and collectibles, the player will collect various currencies that he will use to clear the way of locked portals and will be rewarded for exploration by the ability to disable certain obstacles based on the currency he has obtained on the way. The player will start in the Earth zone and as the story progresses he will face the Fire, Air and Water zones. The main character will learn to master and harness the power of the Elements by completing challenges and exploring the world.

The Team

I am a Informatics Engineering student in Rome and I am doing all of the work in the engine and in the modeling software (Blender). Also on the team are two friend and they're responsible for some of the art. Our approach at creating a cohesive world is procedural generation starting from a hand-crafted sketch and then manually tweaking the result and adding finishing touches. If you wish to join our team on the project we're happy to look at your work/portfolio just email me and I'll reply right away.
